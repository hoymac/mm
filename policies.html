Salon & Shopping Policies

Appointment Booking

At MM HAIR STUDIOS INC., we recommend booking your appointment in advance to ensure availability. Appointments can be made by phone or through our website for your convenience.

Deposits

MM HAIR STUDIOS INC. requires a non-refundable deposit to secure appointments for services such as hair extensions, coloring, and bridal packages. This deposit will be applied toward your final bill.

Cancellations & Rescheduling

Please provide at least 24 hours’ notice for cancellations or rescheduling at MM HAIR STUDIOS INC. Late cancellations or no-shows will result in the loss of your deposit and may impact your ability to book future appointments.

Late Arrivals

We understand that unexpected delays happen. If you are running late, please contact MM HAIR STUDIOS INC. as soon as possible. Arrivals more than 15 minutes late may require rescheduling to avoid impacting other clients’ appointments.

Service Satisfaction Guarantee

Your satisfaction is our top priority at MM HAIR STUDIOS INC.. Please communicate your preferences clearly during your appointment. Any concerns should be addressed within 7 days for adjustments or consultations.

Refund Policy

Due to the personalized nature of our services, MM HAIR STUDIOS INC. does not offer refunds. Deposits are non-refundable. We strive to provide the highest quality services and encourage open communication to meet your expectations.

Health & Safety

  • MM HAIR STUDIOS INC. follows strict sanitation protocols and maintains a clean, safe environment for all clients and staff.
  • Please inform us of any allergies, scalp conditions, or sensitivities prior to your appointment with MM HAIR STUDIOS INC.
  • Clients with contagious conditions (e.g., lice, scalp infections) will be asked to reschedule to protect everyone’s health.

Payment Methods

MM HAIR STUDIOS INC. accepts cash, debit, and major credit cards. Payment is due at the time of service or purchase.

Shop & Purchase Policies

MM HAIR STUDIOS INC. offers a curated selection of hair products and extensions for sale both in-store and online. Please review our product purchase policies below:

  • Product Availability: MM HAIR STUDIOS INC. strives to keep all products in stock; however, availability may vary. Orders are fulfilled on a first-come, first-served basis.
  • Pricing: Prices at MM HAIR STUDIOS INC. are subject to change without notice. Any price changes will not affect orders already confirmed.
  • Returns & Exchanges: Due to hygiene and quality reasons, MM HAIR STUDIOS INC. does not accept returns or exchanges on opened hair extensions or hair products. Unopened items can be returned within 7 days in original condition with receipt.
  • Shipping: Online orders from MM HAIR STUDIOS INC. are shipped within 2-3 business days. Shipping costs and delivery times vary based on location.
  • Damaged or Incorrect Items: Please contact MM HAIR STUDIOS INC. immediately if you receive damaged or incorrect products. We will work to resolve the issue promptly.
  • Warranty: MM HAIR STUDIOS INC. stands behind the quality of our hair extensions. If you experience issues due to manufacturing defects, please contact us within 14 days.
