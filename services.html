Frequently Asked Questions

  • How long do hair extensions last? Properly maintained extensions can last 8–12 weeks depending on the type and care.
  • Is hair coloring damaging? We use gentle, high-quality products that minimize damage and nourish your hair.
  • Can I wash my hair after extensions? Yes, but we recommend waiting 48 hours before washing to ensure secure bonding.
  • Do I need a consultation before services? Yes! We encourage consultations to personalize your experience and ensure your goals are met.

Hair Care Tips

  • Use sulfate-free shampoos and conditioners to maintain color and hair health.
  • Brush extensions gently with a loop brush to avoid damage.
  • Avoid excessive heat styling to prolong your color and extension life.
  • Schedule regular maintenance appointments to keep your hair looking fresh.
